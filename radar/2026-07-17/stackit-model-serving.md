**Update 07/2026:** The service is **generally available since May 2025** (no longer beta) and was renamed to "STACKIT AI Model Serving". The model portfolio is actively maintained (2026 additions include gpt-oss-20b and Qwen3 embedding models; Llama 3.3, Gemma and others are available).

It remains our recommended option for German data-sovereignty requirements - with the known trade-off that the model selection stays limited compared to the hyperscalers.

Updated links: [Product page](https://stackit.com/en/products/data-ai/stackit-ai-model-serving), [Release notes](https://docs.stackit.cloud/products/data-and-ai/ai-model-serving/release-notes/) (the beta URL in the previous entry is outdated).
