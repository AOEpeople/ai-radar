**Update 07/2026 (assess → hold):** The project has effectively gone dormant: the last release (v0.12.0) dates from May 2025, more than 200 issues are open, and the community is openly asking whether development continues ([Releases](https://github.com/Cinnamon/kotaemon/releases), [Discussion](https://github.com/Cinnamon/kotaemon/discussions/778)).

Kotaemon remains a readable reference implementation of RAG patterns (hybrid retrieval, reranking, GraphRAG integration), but we do not recommend it for new deployments. Actively maintained alternatives for self-hosted document chat: [OpenWebUI](/tools/openweb_ui/) and [LibreChat](/tools/librechat/).
