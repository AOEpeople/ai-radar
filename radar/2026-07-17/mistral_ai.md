Mistral AI is the only European frontier provider - and its strategy is to offer the complete **sovereign AI stack**, from infrastructure to application:

- **Compute** - frontier-scale infrastructure for training and inference, backed by own data centers in France.
- **Studio** (formerly La Plateforme) - build, test and run AI agents and apps, with EU data residency by default.
- **Forge** - train, align and evaluate custom AI models.
- **Vibe** - AI agent for long-horizon work (formerly Le Chat), plus **Vibe for code** with coding agents in terminal, IDE and background.

The model lineup spans edge to frontier at aggressive pricing; **open weights** (Apache 2.0 for several models) enable fully self-hosted deployments - the second pillar of the sovereignty story. Funding (€1.7B Series C led by ASML) and enterprise references (Cisco, Stellantis, French government bodies) back the strategy.

First choice when EU sovereignty/GDPR requirements are strict or open-weight self-hosting is wanted; benchmark quality typically sits somewhat behind the US frontier leaders.
