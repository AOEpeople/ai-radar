**Update 07/2026:** Important licensing change: since v0.6.6 (April 2025) OpenWebUI is no longer plain open source. The own "Open WebUI License" (BSD-3 plus branding/anti-endorsement clause, with CLA) is **not OSI-conformant**, and white-labeling requires an enterprise license ([license docs](https://docs.openwebui.com/license/)).

Development itself remains very active (v0.8.x with terminal integration, enterprise OAuth, SBOM attestation). For internal self-hosted chat deployments this changes little - but evaluate the license before any commercial or white-label scenario. [LibreChat](/tools/librechat/) remains a fully MIT-licensed alternative.
