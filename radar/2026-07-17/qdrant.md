**Update 07/2026 (trial → adopt):** Qdrant has proven itself in production and remains one of our first choices when a dedicated, self-hostable vector database is needed - especially for data-sovereign setups.

Qdrant continued strong development: v1.17 added agent-oriented primitives, v1.18 (May 2026) brought TurboQuant quantization and dynamic named vectors ([Releases](https://github.com/qdrant/qdrant/releases)).
